About me

I am a Front-end developer based in Eppelheim, Baden-Württemberg in Germany. To advance my skills in Web Development I have built several Web-Apps and attended an extensive Coding School, where I learned professional operating principles in programming. Feel welcome to visit my public portfolio, which includes games, responsive websites, and business apps.
I started my career in nursing. The intensive care unit was my first ward, where I gained a strong foundation of medical knowledge. Then I switched to a Stroke Unit because I wanted more direct contact with my patients. Working in healthcare has taught me patience, resilience, and adaptability to alleviate the most pressing needs of the people I work with and for.
I like to read fiction and have cookouts with my family and friends. I am open to new challenges and looking for a chance to apply my coding experience as a junior developer.
